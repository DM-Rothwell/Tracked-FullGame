For my Game I used the following assests from the Unity Asset store. 
Theses all fall under the MyAssests folder in my Assets Folder
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
1) Used the standard Assets (for Unity 2018.4): 
	Standard Assets (for Unity 2018.4)
	https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/essentials/asset-packs/standard-assets-for-unity-2018-4-32351
	Deleted files that would not be used.

2) For background buildings: 
	russain_buildings by MadMedicSoft
	https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/3d/environments/urban/russian-buildings-lowpoly-pack-80518
	Deleted buildings that would not be used.

3) Background music: 
	Take a Ride (Lounge Music) by TFSUI
	https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/audio/music/take-a-ride-lounge-music-77066
	Asset remains unchanged from the source.

4) Sound effects
	Free Casual SoundFX Pack by Tim Beek
	https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/audio/sound-fx/free-casual-soundfx-pack-164843
	Deleted sound effects that would not be used.	

5) Particle Effect
	Particle Dissolve Shader Package by Moonflower Carnivore
	https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/vfx/particles/fire-explosions/particle-dissolve-shader-package-33631
	Deleted particles and their properties that will not be used
